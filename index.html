Return & Refund Policy

Thanks for shopping with Afromart!

Key Terms.

1. Cancellation on day of delivery is not possible as all items will have already began processing for delivery. Cancellation of any order must be done before the delivery day.

2. In the event that you reject an order or you are not available to receive your order after it has been dispatched to you, an additional delivery fee will be charged for a second delivery and Afromart is not liable for natural deterioration of perishable items in such cases.

3. While our utmost priority is to ensure our customers&aspos; satisfaction, we acknowledge that there may be instances where items are not delivered in their entirety. This can occur due to delays on the part of our farmers and suppliers or issues related to the quality of specific items supplied. Nevertheless, please rest assured that we are fully committed to making every effort to provide you with complete orders whenever possible.

4. Please be advised that our delivery partners adhere to a strict 15-minute waiting window at designated locations. This policy is in place to ensure timely deliveries to all our valued customers, including those who may be awaiting their orders.

To prevent any potential delays or additional transportation costs, we kindly request your prompt availability to receive your order within this specified timeframe. Your cooperation in this matter is greatly appreciated.

5. We want you to be happy with your purchases, so we offer refunds for both perishable and non-perishable items within six (6) hours of delivery.

To learn more about our refund policy, please read this guide. But please keep in mind that once you've paid for food items, you can't cancel your order on the day of delivery.

If you do need a refund, we'll process it through either your Afromart wallet or your bank account, unless our refund policy says otherwise.

Guidelines for Refund.


N.B: Please note that all returns/refunds will be considered on a case-by-case basis, taking into account the availability of the food item, logistics, and reasonable grounds.

Eligibility for Refund.

Perishable Food Items: Our refund policy for perishable food items covers a variety of products, including fresh tomatoes, fresh peppers, meat, fish, and fruits and vegetables.

If you are unsatisfied with the quality of these items, you may request a refund within six (6) hours of receiving your delivery.

To be eligible for a refund, you must have made a complaint to Afromart on the day of delivery and provided picture or video evidence of the unsatisfactory order.

We typically require visual evidence to ensure that we can process your refund or return promptly.

Non-Perishable Food Items: Our refund policy applies to various food items such as Grains (Rice, Beans, Corn), Roots (Plantain, Potatoes), Tubers (Yams), and other dry items (spices). If you receive any of these items and wish to return them, you must do so within 24 hours of delivery.

However, there is a prerequisite for this return and refund to take place. You need to have lodged a valid complaint within six (6) hours of receiving the food items.

Our team at Afromart will confirm your complaint, and we may request picture evidence to ensure a smooth return or refund process.

Exempted Food Items for Return/Refunds

Under this policy, Afromart will not accept any returns or issue refunds for any food items (regardless of whether they are perishable or non-perishable) unless a valid complaint is filed within 6 hours of receipt of delivery and confirmed by Afromart.

Delayed Refunds

Once Afromart confirms your refund, kindly check your Afromart wallet bank account to confirm if the refund has been processed.

If the refund was sent directly to your bank account, please allow some time for the refund to be officially posted by your bank.


Cost of Transportation for Returns

If food items are defective or the customer has a valid complaint registered with Afromart, the cost of transportation for the return of the items will be covered by Afromart. However, if the customer rescinds or changes an initial order, they will be responsible for bearing the cost of transportation.
